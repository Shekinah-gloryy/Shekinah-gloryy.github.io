Cherished

"His grace and mercy have covered you. You are not the same; no longer bound by what you used to do. That’s why He came—so you could make a change completely now. And in His name, you’re a conqueror, beloved too"

Generes: Fiction, Christian Fiction
Summary: Cherished by Kim Cash Tate follows the lives of Kelli London and Heather Anderson, two women struggling to find their worth and identity in the midst of challenging circumstances. Kelli is a successful but emotionally scarred woman, while Heather is a devoted mother trying to rebuild her life after a painful divorce. As both women confront their pasts and face present-day trials, they learn to embrace God's love and grace, which allows them to heal and discover their true worth. The novel delves into themes of forgiveness, self-worth, and the transformative power of God's unconditional love, showing that regardless of past mistakes, every woman is cherished by God and capable of redemption.
