Generes: Poetry, Family, Death, Fiction
Summary: "A novel that follows a girl named Hannah, who finds herself grappling with deep personal loss while coming to terms with the impact of secrets within her family. As the story unfolds, Hannah navigates the complexities of family dynamics, love, and grief, while uncovering unexpected truths about herself. The novel explores themes of identity, resilience, and the nuances of human relationships, especially in the face of overwhelming sorrow. The characters’ emotional journeys are both intimate and universal, offering a poignant look at the truths we must all confront.​" ​
