Nearly All The Men In Lagos Are Mad

"Happy endings are for mumus and fairy tales. Happy endings are the code name for a special type of massage in brothels.
When it comes to relationships, you are lucky if you get a happy beginning and a not-so-terrible ending."

⭐️⭐️⭐️
Generes: Short stories, Fiction, Nigera, Romance
Summary: A collection of 12 short stories that explore the complexities of modern relationships in Lagos. Each story introduces different characters— career women, side chicks, unfaithful husbands, and manipulative lovers—navigating the chaotic Lagos dating scene. Kuku vividly captures the city's vibrant energy while exposing the absurdities of romantic entanglements. Through relatable yet often outrageous tales, the book offers an entertaining and thought-provoking look at love and relationships in contemporary Nigerian society.
