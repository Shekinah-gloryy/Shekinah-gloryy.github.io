The Rosie Project

"I decided to do the impossible, because the impossible was the only thing that interested me"

⭐️⭐️⭐️⭐️
Generes: Fiction, Romance, Chick Lit
Summary: Follows Don Tillman, a brilliant but socially awkward genetics professor, as he embarks on a quest to find the perfect wife. His solution: create the "Wife Project," a rigorous questionnaire designed to weed out unsuitable candidates. However, his plans are thrown off course when he meets Rosie Jarman, an unpredictable bartender who is nothing like the woman he imagines for himself. As they work together to uncover the identity of Rosie's biological father, Don's rigid worldview begins to shift, leading him to discover that love doesn't follow formulas. The novel is a heartwarming, humorous exploration of love, relationships, and personal growth.
