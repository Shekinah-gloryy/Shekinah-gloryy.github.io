Goodbye Days

"What I don’t understand is how people can keep living after something like this, I’d give anything to get just one more goodbye day.”

⭐️⭐️⭐️⭐️⭐️
Generes: Mental Health, Family, Death, Fiction
Summary: "the story of Carver, a teenage boy grappling with guilt after his three best friends die in a car crash. Struggling with the aftermath, Carver tries to find peace by honoring their memory, but faces the weight of his emotions and the grief of their families. Through a series of "goodbye days," where he connects with his friends' loved ones, Carver learns to heal and accept his role in the tragedy. This heartfelt novel explores themes of loss, forgiveness, and the power of friendship, offering readers a poignant journey through grief and reconciliation.​" ​
