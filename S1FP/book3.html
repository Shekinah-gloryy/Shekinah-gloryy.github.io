Tommorrow and Tommorrow and Tommorrow

"The way to turn an ex-lover into a friend is to never stop loving them,
to know that when one phase of a relationship ends it can transform into something else."

⭐️⭐️⭐️⭐️⭐️
Generes: Coming of Age, Fiction, Romance
Summary: Follows the lives of childhood friends Sam and Sadie, who reconnect in college and go on to create a video game company. Together with their third partner, Marx, they experience massive success, but their journey is marred by personal conflicts and loss. As the trio faces both professional and emotional challenges, their bond is tested by tragedy, including Marx's untimely death. This poignant story explores themes of creativity, ambition, and the complex nature of relationships, especially friendship. It highlights how art, love, and ambition intersect, and the resilience required to move forward despite setbacks​

Refrence Desk
