Everything's Fine

"If I had to choose between keeping you and keeping my pride,
I’d keep my pride. But what’s left of it, after all this?"

Generes: Race, Fiction, Romance
Summary: A thought-provoking debut that tackles race, politics, and identity through the lives of two college classmates, Jess and Josh, who find themselves working together at Goldman Sachs. Jess, a Black woman, faces systemic challenges in the predominantly white and affluent world of finance, while Josh, a conservative white man, brings an opposing political viewpoint. Their budding romantic connection forces them to confront not just their personal differences but also the broader cultural divides at play, especially as the 2016 U.S. election looms. The novel blends humor with sharp social commentary, posing questions about love, privilege, and the moral compromises we make in a polarized world​" ​

Refrence Desk
