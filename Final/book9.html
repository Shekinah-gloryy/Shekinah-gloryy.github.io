Generes: Fiction, Christian Fiction, Romance,
Summary: Faithful by Kim Cash Tate tells the story of three women—Cydney, Dana, and Phyllis—who face significant challenges in their lives, grappling with issues such as betrayal, heartbreak, and the search for purpose. Through their ndividual struggles, they form a deep bond and begin to understand the power of faith, forgiveness, and trusting in God's timing. As they navigate their personal journeys, each woman learns that while life is often difficult and unpredictable, God's faithfulness remains constant, and His plan for them is always greater than their circumstances. The novel highlights themes of healing, resilience, and the importance of community and spiritual growth in overcoming adversity.
